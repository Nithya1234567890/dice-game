Game Rules

  • The game is played by two players.Each player starts with a score of 0.
  • A player rolls the die and adds the number rolled to their current score.
  • The player has the option to continue rolling or end their turn.
  • If a player rolls a 1, they lose all points gained during that turn, and their turn ends. The other player then takes their turn.
  • A player can choose to end their turn by clicking 'hold' button adding them to their final score.
  • The first player who scored 50 points wins the game.
